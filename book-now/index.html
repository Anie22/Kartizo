What areas of Houston do you service?
Our service coverage extends throughout the Greater Houston metropolitan area. From the bustling streets of Downtown to the suburban comfort of The Woodlands, and from the energy corridor of Katy to the coastal charm of Clear Lake, we're there for you. We typically accommodate moves within a 50-mile radius of Houston's center, ensuring comprehensive coverage for our clients throughout the region.
How do you estimate moving costs?
We believe in transparent, comprehensive pricing that takes into account the unique aspects of your move. Our experienced estimators consider the size of your move, distance, service level required, and any special handling needs. After conducting either an in-person or virtual assessment of your space, we provide a detailed quote that outlines all costs involved, ensuring no surprises on moving day.
What safety measures do you take during the moving process?
Safety is paramount at Kartizo Global. Every team member undergoes thorough background checks and regular safety training. We maintain our equipment to the highest standards and implement comprehensive property protection measures. This includes using floor covers and door jamb protectors during every move. When requested, we also follow enhanced safety protocols, including COVID-19 precautions, to ensure everyone's well-being.
Can you help with furniture assembly/disassembly?
Absolutely! Our professional movers are skilled in both the disassembly and reassembly of furniture pieces. We carefully document the original setup, secure all components, and ensure proper hardware labeling. When we reach your new location, we'll reassemble everything according to your preferred floor plan, ensuring each piece is exactly where you want it.
What should I do with items I don't want to move?
We understand the challenge of dealing with unwanted items during a move. Our team can connect you with reputable local donation centers, estate sale services, and recycling programs. We also have partnerships with professional junk removal services and document shredding companies. You can trust us to handle the entire disposal process professionally and responsibly.
Do you move specialty items like pianos or artwork?
Yes, we specialize in moving high-value and delicate items. Our team has extensive training in handling pianos, fine art, antiques, wine collections, and other specialty pieces. Each item receives customized attention with appropriate equipment and protection methods. We understand that these pieces often hold both monetary and sentimental value, and we treat them accordingly.
What happens if something gets damaged during the move?
While we pride ourselves on prevention, we maintain a straightforward claims process should any damage occur. We immediately document any incidents and initiate a prompt claims process. Our team works diligently to arrange professional repair or replacement options, ensuring fair compensation based on declared value. We're committed to quick resolution and complete client satisfaction.
How do you handle corporate relocations differently from residential moves?
Corporate relocations require a unique approach focused on minimizing business disruption. We offer flexible scheduling including after-hours moving options, specialized handling of IT equipment, and comprehensive project management throughout the process. Our team coordinates with all stakeholders, providing regular updates and ensuring a seamless transition that keeps your business running smoothly.
